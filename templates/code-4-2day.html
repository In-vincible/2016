Introduction

Do you still remember the first time you made your computer print “Hello World!”? Still nostalgic about that moment. Do you still remember the first time you made a complete application to execute the idea you always had in mind? It was an amazing moment, Isn’t it? Or wait! Never got a chance to write your own application. Don’t you worry, Here at Code-4-2day we provide with the much needed chance to convert that million dollar idea to your own application. Code-4-2day is a development side coding event where the participants will be making a complete application in language of their choice

Problem Statement:

1. participants are supposed to create a server api.
2. The API's can be made using any language.
3. The API's should be in XML or JSON format.
4. The API's created should be deployed on a local server.

Event Structure:

1. Participants need to collaborate in a team of maximum of 4 people.
2. The API's need to be submitted before freezing point.
3. After freeze point the API's will be evaluated.
On Spot Event
1. Followed by the freeze point, there will be a 12 hours round the clock coding challenge.
2. Some task specific to the API's developed by every team will be given to the teams.
3. Participants can be asked to modify their API's to support additional functionalities or to debug thier opponent's program.

Judging Criteria:

1. Idea
2. Documentation
3. Functionality
4. Marking Scheme and weightage of On the Spot Events will be disclosed after freezing point only.
PS:- Freezing Point 23st Feb, 2016 11:59 PM
