Problem Statement:

For years, designers of industrial, medical, and other isolated systems had limited options when implementing safety isolation: the only reasonable choice was the opto-isolator.
An opto-isolator is a component that transfers electrical signals between two isolated circuits by using light.They prevent high voltages from affecting the system receiving the signal.
Today, digital isolators offer advantages in performance, size, cost, power efficiency, and integration.So the task is to simulate digital isolator.
